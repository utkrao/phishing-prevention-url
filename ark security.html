Phishing is an act attempting to acquire information such as user name password and credit card details as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication. Communications purporting to be from popular social websites, auction sites, online payment process or IT administrators are commonly used to lure the unsuspecting public. Phishing emails may
contain links to website that are infected with malware.

About

Our software is used for the detection of phishing that takes place through e-mails and URLs and prevention of acts like identity theft, theft of important data, theft of official data and of personal documents and files.
This software can help to prevent an attack by shielding employees from suspicious emails, blocking malicious links, stopping weaponized attachments and identifying signs of fraud and impersonation. It is often integrated with web browsers and email clients as a toolbar that displays the real domain name for the website the viewer is visiting, in an attempt to prevent fraudulent websites from masquerading as other legitimate websites.

If the user receives a mail (say in the name of their bank) asking to submit details for regular updates or something as such through a URL then as the user will click on the URL it will direct them to a website. Here our software comes into play, it will detect that the website is really an authentic one or not. If it is authentic then the software will let you proceed and if it is fake then it will send a warning message that this website might be fake.
